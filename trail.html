Medical Training - ISUOG 6 Step Approach

About ISUOG Training

ISUOG (International Society of Ultrasound in Obstetrics and Gynecology) offers comprehensive training programs for medical professionals involved in obstetric ultrasound. Our 6 Step Approach focuses on essential aspects of fetal assessment and helps practitioners develop skills in fetal imaging and diagnosis.

About ISUOG Basic Training

ISUOG Basic Training involves a comprehensive 6-Step Approach designed to equip healthcare professionals with essential skills for obstetric ultrasound assessment.

Learning Objectives

By the end of the training, participants will be proficient in assessing:

  • Fetal lie and presentation
  • Placental localization
  • Amniotic fluid volume (AFV)

Key Questions

Participants will be able to navigate through probe movements to evaluate:

  1. Fetal lie and presentation
  2. Number of fetuses
  3. Placental site
  4. Amniotic fluid volume

Training Benefits

The Basic Training simplifies the otherwise complex obstetric ultrasound process. It establishes standardized approaches to minimize steps, ensure image consistency, and streamline examinations. The program aims to reduce the learning curve while paving the way for automated practices.
